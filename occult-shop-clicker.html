Welcome to your new occult shop.

You've inherited a dusty storefront filled with cursed merchandise, haunted inventory, and a cash register that definitely shouldn't be glowing like that. Click it anyway.

Occult Shop Clicker is an incremental idle game where you run a supernatural shop, accumulate spectral wealth, and progressively unlock deeper layers of cosmic power.

It's a clicker game. You click things. Numbers get bigger. It's satisfying.

Build Your Supernatural Inventory

Tap the register to earn Spectral Coins. Buy generators to earn coins automatically. Buy upgrades to earn more coins. You know how this works.

Stock your shop with increasingly absurd merchandise:

  • Floating Candles They hover. Customers love that.
  • Bottled Wisps Ethically sourced. Mostly.
  • Poltergeist Tappers Ghosts that work the register for you
  • Summoning Circles For the discerning interdimensional shopper
  • Reality Forges When regular merchandise isn't enough

12 generators total. Each weirder than the last.

Three Layers of Prestige

When the numbers get big enough, sacrifice everything for permanent power:

Layer 1

Cult Influence

Reset your shop. Gain influence. Buy upgrades that persist. Do it again, but faster.

Layer 2

Eldritch Favor

Reset your cult. Gain cosmic favor. Unlock deeper powers. The entities are watching.

Layer 3

Pact Seals

Reset everything. Bind reality-altering pacts. Change the rules of the game itself.

FAQ

What kind of game is this?

An incremental clicker/idle game. You click to earn currency, buy things that earn currency automatically, and prestige to reset with bonuses. If you've played Cookie Clicker, you know the format.

How long is it?

As long as you want. The core progression takes several hours. Full completion with all achievements takes considerably longer. It's designed to be played casually over days or weeks.

Is it scary?

No. The occult theme is played for atmosphere and humor, not horror. It's cozy spooky, not actually frightening.

Can I play idle?

Yes. The game accumulates offline progress. You can close it and come back later to collected earnings.

The shop is open. Forever.
